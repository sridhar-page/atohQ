# AtohQ: Tab-by-Tab Feature Guide

AtohQ is a premium Queue Management System (QMS) designed to eliminate physical waiting lines and streamline the patient journey. Below is a comprehensive breakdown of the features available in each tab of the application, explaining how they improve facility operations and the patient experience.

---

## 1. Self-Service Portal (Check-in)
*The front door of your facility. A fast, digital-first experience that eliminates physical lines at the registration desk.*

*   **Instant Patient Registration**: Patients join the queue by simply entering their name and phone number. This 30-second process creates a digital identity in our system, reducing manual data entry for staff.
*   **Intelligent Department Routing**: Patients select the specific service they need (e.g., General Medicine, Dental Clinic, or Pharmacy). AtohQ automatically routes them to the correct queue, ensuring they are seen by the right professional in the order they arrived.
*   **Real-Time Queue Visibility**: The service selection dropdown now displays the current queue length (e.g., "General Medicine - 2 tokens waiting"), allowing patients to see exactly how many people are ahead of them before checking in.
*   **Virtual Token Generation**: Upon registration, the system instantly generates a unique Token ID (e.g., G-106). This token is the patient's "digital place" in line, allowing them to relax elsewhere while their phone or central displays keep them updated.

## 2. Receptionist Dashboard (Operational Control)
*The command center for staff. A high-clarity interface that allows receptionists to manage patient flow with precision and ease.*

*   **Live Queue Visualization**: A real-time, side-by-side view of the "Currently Serving" patient and the "Up Next" queue. This at-a-glance visibility ensures staff are always prepared for the next arrival.
*   **Atomic "Complete & Next" Workflow**: This is our "Efficiency Engine." With a single click, the receptionist marks the current consultation as finished and automatically calls the next person in line. This eliminates the "dead time" between patients.
*   **Start Queue Action**: For the first patient of the day or after a break, the receptionist can trigger "START QUEUE" to instantly call the first token from the waiting list.
*   **No-Show & Efficiency Tracking**: Staff can mark missing patients as "No Show" with one click. AtohQ automatically calculates a live **No-Show Rate** percentage, helping management understand patient adherence and operational efficiency.
*   **Direct & Emergency Registration**: Receptionists can manually register walk-in patients. For critical cases, the **Emergency Protocol** can be toggled to generate an "E-" prefix token, which bypasses the standard sequence to ensure immediate medical attention.
*   **Urgent Case Counter**: A dedicated metrics card tracks the number of **Urgent Served** tokens, providing clear visibility into how many high-priority cases have been successfully handled.

## 3. Specialist Intelligence (Departmental Focus)
*A precision lens for clinicians and department heads. This view provides a high-level overview of specific service nodes.*

*   **Department-Specific Analytics**: Unlike the global admin view, the Specialist tab focuses on a single department (e.g., **General Medicine**). It provides localized metrics for visited, served, and waiting patients.
*   **Local Efficiency Metrics**: Clinicians can track their specific average consultation times and peak session durations, allowing for better personal time management and patient care quality.
*   **Specialist Traffic Analysis**: A dedicated hourly flow chart helps specialists anticipate their busiest times and manage their workstation resources effectively.

## 4. Current Status Hub (Patient Transparency)
*The bridge between patients and providers. A dedicated portal that keeps everyone informed and calm.*

*   **Simplified Token Display**: Tokens are displayed using their full alphanumeric identifier (e.g., **G-101** or **E-105**) in a high-contrast, easy-to-read format, eliminating redundant internal numbers for maximum clarity.
*   **Real-Time Position Intelligence**: Patients don't have to guess how long they have left. The hub provides context-aware messages such as "The next turn is yours" or "There are 2 tokens ahead of you."
*   **Interactive Status Tracking**: Patients can click on their specific token in the list to get detailed "Status Intelligence," including which service desk is handling their case and its current state.

## 5. System Admin Dashboard (Operational Intel)
*The decision-maker's lens. A professional analytics suite that turns queue data into actionable insights for facility management.*

*   **Performance Tracking at Scale**: Get an instant pulse on the facility with metrics like "Visited Today" (total registrations), "Served Today" (total completions), and live "In Queue" counts.
*   **Daily Traffic Visualization**: A professional Area Chart maps out patient arrival trends over a 24-hour cycle. This helps managers identify their "peak hours" and optimize staffing levels accordingly.
*   **Service Efficiency Benchmarking**: AtohQ tracks every consultation down to the second. Admins can view Average Service Times and Session Extremes to identify bottlenecks or training opportunities.
